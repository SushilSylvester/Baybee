Baybee

We, at Prime Toys India Pvt. Ltd., offer a collection of complete baby care products. Today, our Baby Products' brand "Baybee" is recognized for its quality and is a household name in India. Back in 1976, we discovered a potential in delivering smiles and happiness to families across Chennai by fulfilling their demands in baby care and baby needs. We now sell everything under the sun when it comes to baby care and needs. Be it the period of maternity, or helping your baby through their first walk, or when your baby plays and learns or the period when your young loved ones has just started their first journey to school. All our products are manufactured after a scientific research on the physiological and psychological growing needs of the baby, combined with constant inputs from the parents. Our products conform with the highest standards set in Europe and USA, and every product undergoes stringent test before packed. So as to ensure we leave no stone unturned in our quest to deliver smile, happiness and love to our customers via our baby-need products. Our constant effort in growing our product range whilst maintaining the high standard of quality we set for ourselves has endowed us with a lot of love from families and babies. And their loyalty towards us has remained a constant source of motivation to strive better in researching and innovating new products to introduce in the market. This not only adhere to the highest international standards, but also gain preference from our customers when it comes to affordability of our products. All our products are made of food grade material and comply with ISI norms.
